Train historical fencing and HEMA in Tromsø

In Tromsø we train with sword & buckler, montante and longsword. All trainings take place at Borgtun school and are open to beginners.

Everyone who wants to try gets two free trial weeks so they can get an impression of what it's like to train with us. If you want to continue training after this, just sign up for the club - you pay for one semester at a time. You only need regular workout clothes and indoor shoes, all swords, masks, etc. can be borrowed from the club in the beginning. HEMA and historical fencing is an exciting activity with a unique historical element, it's also great exercise!

Mondays - Sword & Buckler

For beginners and advanced
Time: Mondays 19:00 - 21:00
Location: Borgtun school, Borgtunvegen 21, 9010 Tromsø
Instructors: Erik Varberg and Jens Peder Magnussen

Single-handed sword with small shield, a popular weapon combination throughout the Middle Ages.

Wednesdays - Montante

For beginners and advanced
Time: Wednesdays 19:30 - 21:30
Location: Borgtun school, Borgtunvegen 21, 9010 Tromsø
Instructors: Erik Varberg and Jens Peder Magnussen

Large two-handed sword popular in Spain, Italy and Germany from the 16th century, also known as zweihander, spadone and greatsword.

Fridays - Longsword

For beginners and advanced
Time: Fridays 18:00 - 20:00
Location: Borgtun school, Borgtunvegen 21, 9010 Tromsø
Instructors: Erik Varberg and Jens Peder Magnussen

Long sword mostly used with two hands. Used in battle, duels and as a sport from the Middle Ages into the Renaissance. The largest branch within HEMA.
