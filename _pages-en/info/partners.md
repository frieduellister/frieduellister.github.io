## Faits D'Armes

Faits D'Armes is a French online store for HEMA and our preferred supplier of protective equipment.
They have a large selection of equipment, cheap shipping and fast service! As a partner, all our members get
discounts on purchases of equipment from them. See [Faits D'Armes online store](https://faitsdarmes.com/en/)
